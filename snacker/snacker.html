What does snacker do?

Snacker suggests the closest food establishments based on what you want to eat. For example, if you want a milkshake, it tells you where the closest place to get one is.

More info

Who is it for?

Snacker is for anybody who gets hungry once in a while and thinks to themselves "I really want this specific food today". So they open snacker and find the nearest place that has that.
