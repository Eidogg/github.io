What is Snacker?

Snacker is an app, which allows you to search for foods and food establishments based on your taste, price range etc. You can also access every establishments menu on the app.

Why should I use it?

Snacker is very easy to use while on the move and it helps you budget and preplan all you meals at local restaurants. You also have the option to order your food upfront so its ready when you get there!

What are its main features?

The main features include:
- Searching for food item or restaurants
- Seeing restaurant menus
- Navigation to restaurant
- Restaurant information
- Restaurant reviews and pictures
- Verified restaurant owner can regularly update menu and prices
- Ordering a food item from the app so it is ready when you get there.
- Option to see whether foods are marked vegan, gluten free etc by the restaurant owner.
