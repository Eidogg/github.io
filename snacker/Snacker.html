What does snacker do?

Snacker suggests the closest food places based on what you want to eat. For example, if you want a milkshake, it tells you where the closest place to get one is. The problem it solves is when a person is craving a certain food, they often dont know where to get said food or what the competitors nearby are. Similar apps exist for food delivery systems, but Snacker would just be a simple map based app that would be easy to use and navigate.

Who is it for?

Snacker is for anybody who gets hungry once in a while and thinks to themselves "I really want this specific food today". So they open snacker and find the nearest place that has that. Our target demographic is mostly young adults and our early adopters will most likely be teens and young adults who know their way around technology.
