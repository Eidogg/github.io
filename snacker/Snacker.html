App description

My app would suggest the closest food places based on what you want to eat. For example, if you want a milkshake, it tells you where the closest place to get one is. The problem it solves is when a person is craving a certain food,
but doesnt know where to get said food (also google search or similar doesnt suggest the closest or best place, just if it finds the keyword you search for then it will maybe suggest something vaguely similar). Similar apps exist for food delivery systems,
but my app would just be a simple map based app that would be easy to use and navigate.
