How do you play?

You play as a brave knight, who has to find their way out of some caves.
The dark caves offer many paths, but there is only one way out.
You must choose which path you want to take and hope
that god is on your side, because the path may
end up leaving you lost and dead.


As you leap into the darkness, hoping to land on a platform,
your heart skips a beat. But the platform is there and your
venture continues. The only light in these moldy caves is your
angelic glow, which you must use to find your way out. The game
is much like a maze, but when you fail many times in a row, then the
gods may show mercy upon you.
